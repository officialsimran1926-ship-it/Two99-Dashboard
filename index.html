Leave Policy Summary

Earned / Casual Leave
12 days per FY · Min 7 days notice · Up to 30 days carry forward
Sick Leave
6 days per FY · Medical cert needed for 2+ days · No carry forward
Bereavement Leave
3 days per incident · Immediate family only · Within 15 days of death
⚠ Sandwich Rule
Fri + Mon leave = 4 days deducted (Sat & Sun included). WFH is NOT a substitute for leave.
Apply via email to HR (hr@two99.org) with TL + COO in CC · Approval at Reporting Manager's discretion
